Executive Statement

Executive Alignment

This section shows how your three entities — Face Down Prayer, Women of Travail, and Alpha Omega Chi — connect as one unified movement. Led by Bishop Donald L. & Evangelist Lady Barbara A. Golder from Temple of Praise Assembly in Indianapolis, IN, these three entities are already operating as a single spiritual ecosystem with chapters in 40+ states and 7 countries. Understanding this alignment is the foundation for everything that follows in this roadmap.

Face Down Prayer

The International Face Down Prayer Movement

The spiritual posture and core prayer lifestyle anchoring the entire movement. Chapters across 40+ states and 7 countries unite on the first Saturday of every month at 10:00am EST for global corporate prayer.

Women of Travail

Annual 8-Week Prayer Empowerment Course

A free annual 8-week prayer and empowerment course for women of all ages, ethnicities, and backgrounds. Offered in-person at Temple of Praise Assembly and virtually via Facebook Live. 2026 cohort: Feb 24 – Apr 14 (Tuesdays, 7–8:30pm EST).

Alpha Omega Chi

Christian Sorority, Inc. — Founded 2015

Founded in the Dayton/Cincinnati, OH area, AOCCSI is a Christian sorority built on a foundation of Face Down Prayer. Its purpose: eradicate the social, economic, emotional, physical, and spiritual disparity affecting women and girls worldwide.
